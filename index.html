Introduction

I am an ELLIS PhD student in the IMAGINE computer vision team of École des Ponts ParisTech (ENPC) and in the Perceiving Systems Department of Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems (MPI-IS).

I am co-advised by Gül Varol (ENPC) and Michael J. Black (MPI).

My PhD topic is to generate realistic and diverse human body motion in a controllable way (given labels or text instructions)
