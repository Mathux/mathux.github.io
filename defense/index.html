PhD Defense

Organization

My PhD defense will take place:

  • on April 17th, 2024
  • at 2pm (Paris time)
  • at Ecole des Ponts ParisTech (Cité Descartes, 8 Av. Blaise Pascal 6 et, 77420 Champs-sur-Marne)
    • Please follow the map below to find the correct entrance (Bd Copernic)
  • in Amphi NAVIER (Carnot building)

The defense will be followed by a celebration ("pot de thèse") in the Coriolis building (4th floor) starting whenever the defense is over – people joining for the pot can aim at around 4:30pm.

Directions

To get there, the easiest is with the RER A. Get off at the Noisy-Champs station. Then at the head end of the train, take the exit on the right (exit #3 named 'Bd Newton, Cité Descartes'). Then follow the map to get into the ENPC (Bd Copernic), then to the Amphi NAVIER and/or to the Pot de thèse.

Online version

If you cannot make it to the ENPC, you can also follow the defense on YouTube.

Night celebration

After the defense, we will go to a bar in Paris at around 7pm. I will give more details later on this webpage.
